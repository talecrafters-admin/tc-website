## Free SEO Tools

### Google Analytics

- **Overview**: Essential for tracking website performance, user behavior, and conversion rates.
- **Key Features**: Real-time data, audience insights, and conversion tracking.

### Google Search Console

- **Overview**: A must-have tool for monitoring your website’s presence in Google search results.
- **Key Features**: Performance reports, URL inspection, and sitemap submission.

### Ubersuggest

- **Overview**: A versatile tool for keyword research and competitive analysis.
- **Key Features**: Keyword suggestions, content ideas, and backlink data.

### Answer The Public

- **Overview**: Great for discovering questions and queries your target audience is asking.
- **Key Features**: Visual keyword data, content ideas, and search listening.

### SEOquake

- **Overview**: A browser extension that provides on-the-fly SEO insights.
- **Key Features**: On-page SEO audit, keyword density report, and social metrics.

## Paid SEO Tools

### Ahrefs

- **Overview**: A comprehensive tool for backlink analysis and competitor research.
- **Key Features**: Site Explorer, Keywords Explorer, and Content Explorer.
- **Price**: Starts at $99/month.

### SEMrush

- **Overview**: An all-in-one marketing toolkit for SEO, PPC, and content marketing.
- **Key Features**: Domain analysis, keyword research, and site audit.
- **Price**: Starts at $119.95/month.

### Moz Pro

- **Overview**: Provides powerful tools for SEO insights and analytics.
- **Key Features**: Keyword Explorer, Link Explorer, and site audits.
- **Price**: Starts at $99/month.

### Screaming Frog SEO Spider

- **Overview**: A robust tool for technical SEO audits.
- **Key Features**: Site crawling, broken link checking, and XML sitemaps.
- **Price**: Free for up to 500 URLs, £149/year for the paid version.

### SpyFu

- **Overview**: Excellent for competitor analysis and keyword research.
- **Key Features**: Competitor keyword research, PPC analysis, and backlink tracking.
- **Price**: Starts at $33/month.
